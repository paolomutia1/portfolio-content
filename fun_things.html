There will also be alot of signs saying you should drink and party responsbily, which you totally should. You'll see alot of people wearing Umich gear. But, if I'm being honest, I've never been to a football game or tailgate party. I got season tickets for the football games last year, but never got around to attending a game where you are crowded next to a bunch of drink college students. Pride night! Hands down, this is where you need to be for Fall Break!


My friends and I rented an AirBnb very last minute. Don't do what we did! We ended up in the middle of the gun range! I did end up learning how to shoot a rifle by the end of the weekend, but still, I didn't need to watch a horry movie with my friends to be scared because we felt like we were in one already.


Anyway, fall break is usually when Pride night happens in Tranverse City! There is this one bar in the entire city that everyone will go to And trust me, it is so much fun! Definitely beats the Friday Gay nights at Necto, which I haven't attended since I was in college here. Big No!


But what made that night very memorable for me were the incredibly cheap drinks, like $5 for a Moscow Mule? Yes please!


When I heard that price, I turned to my friend and both our eyes widened as if it was about to pop out our heads. Then we pulled out cards and ordered some more. Drinks are cheap during Pride Night at Traverse City!

Bonfires and smores, I mean what could go wrong? Have a tall glass of spiked cider on the side, some good music and great company and you are in for a memorable night filled with laughter.


Okay, so I threw a surprise birthday house party for a really good friend of mine, David. I basically invited his entire graduate cohort. I don't know how we fit in the house but we did! The party was amazing because I hosted it, I had desserts ready, drinks, and warm appetizers ready for everyone. I sectioned it in different parts of the house that each had different themes and music going on.


It was a whole experience! So, if you ever hear that I'm throwing a party. You must come!


Anyway, the bonfire and smores is just the best fall thing to do! Especially at a party! at the end of the night when everyone is gone and all the people that are left and the real ones, you must light the wood and being the bonfire ritual. I feel like bonfires a great place to start conversations and get to know someone. You can tell funny stories to each other about what happened during the party, who got the most drunk. But no matter what, the conversation keeps going until the fire dies.


While the conversations are going on, you can also take a little skewer and place a marshmallow on it and toast it. After that, you can take the graham crackers and the Hershey chocolate bar and create a delicious dessert called a Smore! It's warm, sweet, gooey, and it feels like someone just gave you a hug. Delicious! I thought I fell in love with a girl when I saw her eat one.
